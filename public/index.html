Covid-19 tracker

As the virus that causes Covid-19 spreads worldwide, this website offers a snapshot of the crisis right now. Turn on your location to get Latest covid 19 data and news about your country.

search country name in search bar to get more detail about covid 19 status, or see the full countries list.

You can also get info about countries by clicking on countries at worlds Map.

The datasets are drawn from over 6 sources and can be reviewed here. In some cases, data on hospitalizations were not available.
